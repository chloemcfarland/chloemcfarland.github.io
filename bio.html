Biography

Chloe McFarland is a photographer and graphic designer from Baltimore, MD, currently based in Chicago, IL. She enjoys making fine art photography work centered around navigating the world as a young person in the 21st century, and the emotional turmoil that comes as a result. When she is not photographing or designing, she enjoys cooking, coloring, working out, and cuddling with her cat.
